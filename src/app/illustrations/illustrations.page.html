Types of illustrations and when to use them

  • Use spot illustrations for
    • Onboarding
    • Price breakdowns
    • When icons aren't enough
    • Guiding the user
    • Marketing and special moment modals
  • Use scene illustrations for
    • 404 error pages
    • Empty states

When to not use illustrations

  • Sunsetting a feature? More appropriate: introducing something new in place of it.
  • If you’re not sure an illustration will be utilized in your flow (come to us when you’re ready, or let’s chat if you’ve got some ideas).
