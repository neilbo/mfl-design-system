WHAT EVEN IS A DESIGN SYSTEM?

It is a set of rules or guidelines that heightens the level of harmony in a digital ecosystem. QIKKIDS is growing, a lot. One of the challenges facing us as the business evolves is maintaining a consistent, cohesive and captivating experience across our digital ecosystem.

We’ve been working to create this design system as a centralised hub for showcasing our design rules, principles, and frontend components. It’s a place for people familiar with the QIKKIDS ecosystem, and for anyone wishing to learn more about our work.

GOALS

Living, cohesive design language An update to any component in the design system will roll out across every QIKKIDS application, reducing variation and inconsistency in experience across our interface. More efficiencies This single-source approach to building out our interface means we can create and iterate faster, whilst empowering engineers to implement design changes and deliver features with minimal upfront design work.

Tested, and trustworthy The design system has been tried and tested, it’s scalable to all devices, and strives to be AA compliant with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. Onboarding Each designer and engineer will work with the most up to date UI patterns, principles, and guidelines to maintain cohesion across the teams.
